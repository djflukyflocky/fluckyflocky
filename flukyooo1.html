About Me

I am DJ Fluky Flocky, a versatile DJ specializing in various genres including Hiphop, Trap, Drill, French, Amapiano, Gengetone, and Arbantone. With a passion for music and a knack for mixing the perfect beats, I bring energy and rhythm to every event I perform at.
